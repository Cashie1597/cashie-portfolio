How it works

A short, honest process.

No drawn-out discovery decks. We agree on the smallest useful version, I build it, you use it.

STEP 01

Talk

A quick chat about the problem and what "done" looks like. I send back a fixed scope and price.

STEP 02

Shape

I sketch the flow and key screens early, so we're aligned before any real building starts.

STEP 03

Build

I build in short passes and share progress as it goes — no month-long silence, no surprises.

STEP 04

Hand over

You get the working thing, plain instructions, and a short window for tweaks once it's live.
