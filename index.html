How it works

A simple request-first process.

You send the details, I scope the smallest useful version, then I build it in clear passes.

STEP 01

Request

You tell me what you need using the request form. I review it and decide whether it is a good fit.

STEP 02

Scope

If it is a fit, I send back a proposed scope, timeline, and fixed quote.

STEP 03

Build

I build in short passes and share progress as I go.

STEP 04

Hand-off

You get the working result, plain setup instructions, and a short window for final tweaks.
