About

An independent software studio.

Cashie.dev is an independent software studio based in Sydney, Australia. I build native macOS apps, Linux applications, internal dashboards and workflow automation — focused tools that ship and stay in use.

Not a freelancer. Not an agency. Not a startup. I'm an independent software builder who designs, builds and maintains real software that solves specific problems.

Every product I build starts with a simple question: what's the smallest useful version? Then I build it, ship it, and keep using it. If it doesn't earn its place in my own workflow, it doesn't ship.

FAQ

Quick questions.

If you don't see your question here, start a project and I'll answer directly.

What platforms do you build for?

macOS and Linux are my main focus — native apps, menu bar utilities, dashboards, and automation tools. I don't currently build mobile apps or Windows-only software.

Do you build mobile apps?

No. I don't take iOS or Android projects. If your idea could work as a desktop tool or internal dashboard, I'm happy to look at it.

How much does a project cost?

Most starter projects fall between A$500 and A$3,500. Larger or more complex systems are quoted after a short scope review. Everything is fixed price and agreed before work starts.

How long does a build take?

Small tools and scripts often take a few days to a week. Dashboards and desktop apps typically take 1–3 weeks. I'll give you a clear timeline with the quote.

What happens after I submit a request?

I'll review the scope and come back with whether it's a fit, a suggested approach, and a fixed price. If we agree, I build in short passes and share progress as it goes.
